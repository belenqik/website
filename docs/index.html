Belén Link

Artist. Designer. Creator.
I am inspired by sci-fi and fantasy; a theme that is quite prevalent throughout my shop. But I also love nature (botany and universe ) which has a strong presence as well. I currently spend most of my time in my studio in Portland, Oregon. I delving into the world of copper and polymer clay arts which has given me the tools I need to create my craft. From accessories like incense holder, Shodō paper holder and jewelry to tea pet I can make all of my favorite design with nothing but my hands, some natural original pears sea, gold paper and some knots. For some pieces I use the techniques such as hammering, patina, and fire & environment. always wanting my creations to be as beautiful, detailed, and realistic as possible. I hope you appreciate my handcrafted pieces.
